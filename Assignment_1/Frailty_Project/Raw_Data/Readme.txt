Metadata for Frailty_data.csv

Description: This dataset consists the data of 10 female participants, which focuses on the grip strength and frailty status.

This is collected to observe the corelation between the grip strength and frailty.

This dataset has 5 columns and each column and descrption is mention below:

Columns and Units:
->Height (in): The height of the partcipant in inches.
->Weight (lbs): The weight of the participant in pounds.
->Age (years): The age of the partcipant in years.
->Grip Strength (kg): The grip strength of the partcipant in kilograms, measured using a dynamometer.
->Frailty (Y/N): The presence(Y) or absence(N) of frailty symptoms in the partcipant.

- Grip strength was measured using a hand dynamometer in kilograms (kg)
- Frailty was assessed through a physical health evaluation.
- This dataset is a subset and may require further processing for extended studies.
